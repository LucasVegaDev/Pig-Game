Pig Game's Rules

The game has two players, playing in rounds. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he/she wishes.

Each result result is showed in the "current"score section and is added to his global score. If the player rolls a 1, all his round score gets lost! After that, it's the next player's turn.

The player can choose to hold, which means that his round score gets added to his global score. After holding, it the next player's turn.

The first player to reach 100 points wins the game!
